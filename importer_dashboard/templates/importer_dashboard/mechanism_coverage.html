Interpretation Guide
Usability Categories:
  • Fully Usable: All species in the reaction are identified and have thermodynamic data. These reactions can be used in simulations immediately.
  • Partially Identified: Some species are identified, but not all. Focus on identifying the remaining species to unlock these reactions.
  • Not Yet Usable: None of the species in the reaction are identified. These reactions are currently unavailable for simulations.
Recommendations:
  • Sort the species queue by "Most Important (by reactions)" to prioritize species that appear in many reactions.
  • Identifying key species can dramatically increase mechanism coverage by unlocking multiple reactions at once.
  • Aim for at least 80% coverage to ensure comprehensive mechanism representation in simulations.
