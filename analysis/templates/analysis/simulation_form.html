About PyTeCK

PyTeCK (Python Testing of Chemical Kinetics) evaluates the accuracy of kinetic models by comparing predicted behavior against experimental measurements.

Lower error function values indicate better agreement between model and experiment.

Experiment Types
IDT Ignition Delay Time
LFS Laminar Flame Speed
SR Speciation (JSR/Flow Reactor)
Tips

  • Select multiple experiments to compare across conditions
